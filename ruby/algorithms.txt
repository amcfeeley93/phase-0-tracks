Release 0: Equip Your Robot

For each button in the elevator:
  -Identify the buttons that are labeled with a number
      -Count all of these buttons in order from left to right
      -When the the row ends skip to the next row and repeat step 2
      -Continue until desired number is reached

Release 1: Find a Real-Life Algorithm

Getting from Point A to Point B in the shortest amount of time

For each possible route:
  - Identify distance
  - Identify shortest route duration
    - While choosing the shortest route check for traffic
        -If traffic, choose next route
        -If no traffic choose that route
